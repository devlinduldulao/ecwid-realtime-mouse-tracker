Merchant Dashboard

Realtime Mouse Tracker for Ecwid

This Ecwid app is designed for the store owner dashboard, not for shoppers. It gives merchants a live operations view, preview scenarios, and a same-browser self-test flow without needing a database, Redis, or a long-running Node.js server.
